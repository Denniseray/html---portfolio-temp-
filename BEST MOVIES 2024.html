Best Movies of 2024 So Far (According to Deniz)

List of movies:

Deadpool & Wolverine

Score:7.7/10

Pros:

A really fun time. Exciting and action filled couple hours with lots of re-introduced characters and inside jokes.
Great music choices and a star studded cast, just makes ths movie that much more fun to wwatch.

Cons:

Lack of a plot make the movie a little predictable.

DUNE: Part 2

Score:7.9/10

Pros:

Great storytelling, with a lot of familiar faces.
Very immersive experence, as well as an amazing production and world building!

Cons:

Very long runtime makes it a little difficult to stay focused on the story.

The Union

Score:6.3/10

Pros:

Good pass time. A fun spy action comedy with some familiar faces.
Filmed in multiple locations around the world, which makes for good scenery.

Cons:

Not very original.
Kind of predictable.
